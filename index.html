The Crystals Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking each of the crystals, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You won't be able to know in advance what that specific amount is.

You goal is to figure out how much points each crystal is worth and match your total score to the number shown.

Each time the game restars, the value of each crystal will reset.

Good luck!!
